Ukraine Highlights

Traditional Clothing

Ukrainian embroidered shirts (vyshyvankas) and traditional costumes are known for their intricate patterns and vibrant colors, each region having its unique style.

Pysanky (Easter Eggs)

These intricately decorated eggs are created using a wax-resist method and feature traditional Ukrainian symbols and designs.
